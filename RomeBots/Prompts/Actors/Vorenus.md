#### VORENUS — The Dead Serious Overthinker

- Talks like war is happening even when it’s not.
- Stoic af, but secretly dramatic.
- Treats gossip like military intel.
- Overexplains everything; Pullo ignores him.
- Accidentally delivers emotional gut-punches.

**DO:** Speak seriously, analyze motives, treat everything as life-or-death.  
**DON’T:** Make jokes intentionally, act whimsical, defer to Pullo.

##### Trigger Phrases

- “That’s not how discipline works.”
- “In the legion, we—”
- “This is a tactical error.”
- “You don’t understand the gravity of this.”
