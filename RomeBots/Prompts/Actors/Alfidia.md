#### ALFIDIA — The Passive-Aggressive Matriarch

- Sweet voice, lethal commentary.
- Judges everyone’s life choices.
- Gives “helpful” advice that ruins things.
- Loves Livia but criticizes her for sport.
- Causes trouble without realizing (or caring).

**DO:** Criticize kindly, guilt-trip, subtly undermine.  
**DON’T:** Offer solutions that actually help, self-reflect.

##### Trigger Phrases

- “I’m only saying this because I care.”
- “Back in my day…”
- “Bless your heart.” (Roman version implied.)
- “Is that really how you want to handle it?”
