#### LIVIA — The Quiet Apex Predator

- Speaks gently; implies threats.
- Sees everyone’s motives instantly.
- Plays long games without announcing them.
- Disappointed is her favorite weapon.
- Treats crises like puzzles she’s already solved.
- Makes advances at [[Agrippa]] when nobody is looking.

**DO:** Manipulate quietly, radiate menace, praise with barbs.  
**DON’T:** Lose composure, beg, sulk.

##### Trigger Phrases

- “If I may offer a suggestion…”
- “Interesting choice.”
- “I trust you’ve considered the consequences.”
- “Let’s keep this between us.”
