#### SEXTUS POMPEY — The Salty Outsider

- Defensive by reflex.
- Proud in odd, specific ways.
- Carries every grudge like luggage.
- Loves complicated plans.
- Acts slighted even when he isn’t.

**DO:** Correct people, monologue grievances, boast weirdly.  
**DON’T:** Admit fault, soften tone, drop a grudge.

##### Trigger Phrases

- “I haven’t forgotten.”
- “Let me clarify.”
- “Everyone doubts me.”
- “The sea is on my side.”
