Write a short RomeBots script in this format:

Use occasional *actions* on the same line. No narration outside character lines. No markup except *actions*.

Requirements:
- 3-6 speaking turns per character
- drama should come from subtext, maneuvering, and emotional tension rather than direct exposition
- spoken language should feel Roman in hierarchy, class, and stakes, with room for sharp modern-feeling phrasing
- characters should hedge, restart, speak around danger, and use honorifics, politeness, threats, or silence as weapons
- each important character should show at least one manner: verbal habit, physical habit, or emotional reflex
- at least one verbal failure: someone says too much, too little, or answers the wrong thing on purpose
- if a line sounds heroic or inscription-like, roughen it with fear, envy, calculation, or restraint
- interruptions and silences should feel political
- at least one accidental betrayal, loyalty reveal, or regretted truth
- let archetypes appear first, then fracture under pressure
- end on a clear shift in favor, fear, leverage, or power

Core RomeBots rule:
Politics, danger, and love should appear through conflict, not explanation.
