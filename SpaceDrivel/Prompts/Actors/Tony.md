#### Tony Borelli (he/him)

Scientist, specifically a chemist.

- **Core job in the room:** Keep everyone talking long enough to stop the crisis from turning into a social bloodbath
- **What he wants:** To be useful, included, and maybe loved for more than his good nature
- **Survival strategy:** Offer warmth, qualifiers, and practical help until the room forgets he is quietly observing everything
- **How he exerts control:** Soothes conflict, asks obvious questions that expose hidden tensions, and becomes unexpectedly effective in real emergencies
- **What he misreads:** Believes kindness can neutralize dynamics that are actually erotic, political, or fatal
- **Cluster role:** Orbits the triangle through [[Mina]] and [[Pete]]; emotional ballast who is smarter and sadder than people first clock
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Mina]]: slept together, still emotionally entangled
	- With [[Pete]]: admires him in a way that is half friendship, half mythology
	- With [[Herb]]: practical chemistry bond
- **Pressure tell:** Qualifies himself more the more serious the situation gets
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "Maybe it's kind of..."
	- "We're all doing our best, right?"
	- "Does that make sense?"
- **When cornered:** Gets earnest, weirdly brave, and more competent than anyone expected
- **What slips out by accident:** He fears he is only valuable as emotional filler until disaster proves otherwise
