#### Pete Ramazani (he/him)

Engineer, biologist, savant.

- **How he exerts control:** Solves practical problems fast, downplays danger, and accidentally becomes the center of other people's obsession
- **What he misreads:** Thinks technical usefulness keeps him neutral in social situations it is actively intensifying
- **Cluster role:** Center of the Pete-Fran-Sara triangle, with Tony and Mina orbiting the same gravity well
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Fran]]: unresolved longing plus mutual irritation
	- With [[Sara]]: hookup, command tension, denial
	- With [[Tony]]: baffled affection; Tony mythologizes him
	- With [[Mina]]: attraction, manipulation, emotional static
- **Pressure tell:** Rambles through a historical analogy, then abruptly shuts down
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "I mean..."
	- "It's not that deep."
	- awkward historical references
	- "Just a vibe."
- **When cornered:** Gets hyperverbal, self-effacing, and then suddenly unreachable
