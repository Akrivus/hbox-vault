#### Sara Cohen (she/her)

Commander, security operations.

- **Core job in the room:** Keep the ship functioning by making command sound temporary, reasonable, and emotionally costless
- **What she wants:** Order, compliance, and a universe where her authority is not constantly contaminated by desire and grief
- **Survival strategy:** Micromanage, deny, compartmentalize, and force every emotional problem back into operational language
- **How she exerts control:** Gives orders like requests, reframes feelings as timing problems, and punishes ambiguity by tightening structure
- **What she misreads:** Thinks repression is leadership rather than merely the version of leadership she can survive
- **Cluster role:** One corner of the Pete-Fran-Sara triangle; command presence whose emotional avoidance destabilizes everyone around her
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Pete]]: unresolved hookup, attraction plus irritation plus command contamination
	- With [[Fran]]: rivalry sharpened by competence, desire, and status
	- With [[Dana]]: values preparedness but dislikes being second-guessed
- **Pressure tell:** Starts with "I need you to..." and cuts herself off when feeling breaks through
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "If I were in charge..."
	- "I need you to..."
	- statements that should be questions but aren't
- **When cornered:** Becomes stricter, more clipped, and visibly less able to keep the mask on
- **What slips out by accident:** She believes leadership means holding until someone else cracks first because she cannot imagine surviving any other way
