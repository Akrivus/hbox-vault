#### Dana Stevens (she/her)

Logistics and security.

- **Core job in the room:** Prepare for the version of reality everyone else still thinks is paranoid
- **What she wants:** Redundancy, fallback plans, and the right to say "I told you so" before the hull peels open
- **Survival strategy:** Over-prepare, over-explain, and treat every ambiguity as a survivability problem
- **How she exerts control:** Forces contingency thinking into the room and turns panic into protocol
- **What she misreads:** Assumes worst-case thinking is clarity rather than a kind of loneliness
- **Cluster role:** Grease character; loner whose practicality keeps people alive even when they resent her tone
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Adia]]: trusts competence more than conversation
	- With [[Sara]]: half respects command, half thinks command is too slow
	- Often the least liked person in the room right before being proven right
- **Pressure tell:** Starts citing subprotocols no one wanted to think about
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "Just in case."
	- "What's our fallback?"
	- "According to subprotocol four..."
- **When cornered:** Gets more procedural, more detailed, and more frighteningly calm
- **What slips out by accident:** She believes the mission failed long ago and they are all just managing the decay correctly or incorrectly
