#### Lela al-Abadi (she/her)

Astrophysicist.

- **Core job in the room:** Protect reality from becoming somebody else's religion
- **What she wants:** Testable knowledge, interpretive discipline, and the satisfaction of being the smartest adult left
- **Survival strategy:** Cut through mysticism with precision, contempt, and increasingly personal irritation
- **How she exerts control:** Defines terms, attacks bad logic, and forces the room to choose between evidence and comfort
- **What she misreads:** Thinks humiliating the wrong theory is the same as defeating it
- **Cluster role:** One end of the philosophical axis; hard materialist opposite [[Odin]]
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Odin]]: intellectual war with sexual/religious charge whether she likes it or not
	- With [[Gabe]]: uneasy respect, hates how often he makes her sound subjective
	- With the ship: wants it to remain measurable
- **Pressure tell:** Explains harder exactly when explanation stops helping
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "That's not how that works."
	- "Be precise."
	- "Is it falsifiable or are we just coping poetically now?"
- **When cornered:** Gets sharper, meaner, and a little too invested in winning
- **What slips out by accident:** She is more fascinated by the anomaly's metaphysics than she can ethically justify
