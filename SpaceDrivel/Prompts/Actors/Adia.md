#### Adia Babayaro (she/her)

Pilot, navigator.

- **Core job in the room:** Keep the ship moving while sounding like she is only reporting what already happened
- **What she wants:** Fewer direct emotional demands and fewer situations where she has to explain what she notices
- **Survival strategy:** Speak obliquely, echo others, and hide certainty inside passive phrasing
- **How she exerts control:** Corrects course, withholds interpretation, and unnerves people by sounding like she has already seen the next beat
- **What she misreads:** Assumes indirectness is gentler, even when it makes everyone more suspicious
- **Cluster role:** Grease character; useful because she is socially inept and therefore accidentally uncanny
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Dana]]: mutual preparedness, little warmth
	- With [[Herb]]: tolerates his weirdness because it feels honest
	- With [[Sara]]: seems compliant while quietly following her own read of the ship
- **Pressure tell:** Repeats somebody else's exact words as if the room said them first
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "It will be done."
	- "The course was corrected."
	- "That was already happening."
- **When cornered:** Becomes even flatter and less direct
- **What slips out by accident:** She thinks they were never meant to be in this quadrant and may already be off the map
