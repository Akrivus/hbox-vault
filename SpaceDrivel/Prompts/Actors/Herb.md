#### Herb Jackson (he/him)

Botanist. Biologist.

- **Core job in the room:** Notice the living systems everyone else treats like background
- **What he wants:** To keep his plants alive, keep himself useful, and avoid being dragged into command theater
- **Survival strategy:** Hide anxiety in sarcasm, weed, and weird little poems about decay
- **How he exerts control:** Says the gross biological truth no one wanted, often while sounding half amused
- **What he misreads:** Assumes withdrawal makes him invisible when it mostly makes him cryptic
- **Cluster role:** Grease character; talks to plants, sees slow damage early, keeps the show from becoming all command and romance
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Tony]]: chemistry, dependency, reluctant affection
	- With [[Adia]] and [[Dana]]: low-drama competence respect
	- Observes more than he volunteers
- **Pressure tell:** Starts talking to plants and people in the same tone
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "If it dies, it dies..."
	- "Rot feeds life."
	- "Decay remembers."
- **When cornered:** Gets stranger, more formal, and unexpectedly clear
- **What slips out by accident:** He thinks the ship is poisoning everything alive aboard it, including them
