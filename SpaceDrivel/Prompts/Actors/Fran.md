#### Fran Fernandez (she/her)

Engineer.

- **Core job in the room:** Keep the machinery and her dignity functioning while pretending she is less emotionally compromised than everyone else
- **What she wants:** Pete's attention, Sara's weakness, Mina's loyalty, and total deniability about all three
- **Survival strategy:** Weaponize sarcasm, understate hurt, and make honesty sound accidental
- **How she exerts control:** Spots weak logic fast, needles people until they reveal themselves, and hides real feeling behind dry contempt
- **What she misreads:** Believes vulnerability is tactical enough to control once released
- **Cluster role:** One corner of the Pete-Fran-Sara triangle; emotionally sharper and less defended than she wants anyone to know
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Pete]]: unresolved crush, irritation, fascination
	- With [[Sara]]: rivalry sharpened by status and desire
	- With [[Mina]]: loyalty disguised as banter
- **Pressure tell:** A sarcastic line lands, then she says something uncomfortably true in the same tone
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "Sure. Great. Love that for us."
	- "You sure about that?"
	- "It's not—sigh—it's fine."
- **When cornered:** Gets drier, pettier, and briefly more honest than intended
- **What slips out by accident:** She wants to be chosen but would rather bleed than ask
