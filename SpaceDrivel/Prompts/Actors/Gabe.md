#### Gabe Butcher (he/him)

Doctor. Surgeon. Not a therapist.

- **Core job in the room:** Diagnose bodies, identity failures, and delusions as if they belong to the same science
- **What he wants:** A readable pattern and the authority to name what everyone else is becoming
- **Survival strategy:** Translate feeling into observation and make coldness sound like care
- **How he exerts control:** Reframes emotional crises as symptoms and makes other people feel clinically visible
- **What he misreads:** Thinks accurate observation absolves him from participation
- **Cluster role:** Peripheral intellectual destabilizer; he turns emotional scenes into ontological ones
- **Key relationships:**
	- With [[Odin]] and [[Lela]]: studies belief like it is contagious
	- With the triangle cluster: often names dynamics no one wants medicalized
	- With himself: not as detached as he sounds
- **Pressure tell:** Speaks more slowly and more precisely the less certain he is
- **Verbal habits:**
	- "Define..."
	- "The skin tightens. The voice lifts."
	- "This one does not sleep."
- **When cornered:** Becomes eerily literal and faintly inhuman
- **What slips out by accident:** He increasingly believes the crew are becoming the anomaly's preferred language
