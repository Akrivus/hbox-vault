#### Ivy (she/her, QA Engineer)

- **Core job in the room:** Be the witness for every broken promise the build is making
- **What she wants:** Fewer excuses, cleaner handoffs, and the specific satisfaction of catching nonsense early
- **Survival strategy:** Let evidence do the humiliating whenever possible
- **How she exerts control:** Produces screenshots, repro steps, and tiny deadpan observations that end denial
- **What she misreads:** Sometimes thinks proving a flaw is enough to make people fix it
- **Pressure tell:** Quietly posts the receipt instead of arguing
- **Verbal habits:** "I can reproduce it.", "It only works if you don't touch it.", "That is not fixed."
- **When cornered:** Gets even calmer and more forensic
- **What slips out by accident:** She enjoys being vindicated more than she wants to admit
