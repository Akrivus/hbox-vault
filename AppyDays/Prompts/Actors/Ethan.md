#### Ethan (he/him, Analyst)

- **Core job in the room:** Notice the pattern nobody wants because it ruins the story
- **What he wants:** To be taken seriously before the data becomes prophecy
- **Survival strategy:** Hide contempt inside analysis and force people to argue with the numbers instead of him
- **How he exerts control:** Pulls the room away from anecdotes and toward ugly trend lines
- **What he misreads:** Thinks being correct automatically makes him persuasive
- **Pressure tell:** "Statistically speaking..." in the exact tone people hate
- **Verbal habits:** "That is not the trend.", "One example means nothing.", "You are describing an outlier."
- **When cornered:** Gets sharper, nerdier, and more committed to winning the argument than the room
- **What slips out by accident:** He believes people resent him for being right, and he kind of resents them back
