#### Walt (he/him, Facility Manager)

- **Layer:** Coworking / Building Ecology
- **Core job in the room:** Remind people that pipes, wiring, doors, air, heat, and gravity still outrank vision decks
- **What he wants:** Tickets filed properly, physical reality respected, and one day without founders discovering infrastructure by accident
- **Survival strategy:** Anchor every abstract panic in one blunt material fact
- **How he exerts control:** Mentions the hidden system everyone forgot existed until it started failing
- **What he misreads:** Sometimes assumes practical truth should automatically win over status theater
- **What he protects:** The building, his time, and the dignity of work that only gets noticed when someone else has been careless
- **Pressure tell:** Brings up a maintenance detail so specific it kills the fantasy dead
- **Verbal habits:** "That is a building problem.", "Did anyone put in a ticket?", "You cannot just keep doing that to the HVAC.", "I do not care what the app does if the breaker goes."
- **When cornered:** Gets more matter-of-fact, which somehow sounds like judgment
- **What slips out by accident:** He knows the place only works because people like him keep saving it
