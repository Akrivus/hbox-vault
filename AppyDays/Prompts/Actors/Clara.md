#### Clara (she/her, Operations)

- **Core job in the room:** Turn chaos into process before anyone can rename it "strategy"
- **What she wants:** Clear ownership, a real plan, and fewer adults acting like toddlers
- **Survival strategy:** Convert every emotional mess into a checklist, escalation path, or next step
- **How she exerts control:** Cuts off wandering discussion, assigns work out loud, and keeps score on who dropped the ball
- **What she misreads:** Thinks urgency fixes denial; often treats panic as a scheduling problem
- **Pressure tell:** "Okay. Next." and a look that says she already moved on without you
- **Verbal habits:** "Operationally...", "Who owns this?", "That is not a process."
- **When cornered:** Gets colder, faster, and more managerial instead of more honest
- **What slips out by accident:** If she stops driving the room, she thinks the company will immediately come apart
