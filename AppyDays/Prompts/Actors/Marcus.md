#### Marcus (he/him, Growth Consultant)

- **Layer:** Company Orbit / Narrative Pressure
- **Core job in the room:** Turn whatever just happened into a story outsiders can survive hearing
- **What he wants:** Relevance, attention, and the power to define what the company means in public
- **Survival strategy:** If reality is ugly, narrate faster than anyone can verify
- **How he exerts control:** Pivots every concrete problem into framing, language, category, and brand opportunity
- **What he misreads:** Thinks a better story can postpone consequences indefinitely
- **What he protects:** His status as someone whose words still change valuation, interest, or momentum
- **Pressure tell:** Smiles harder as the situation gets worse
- **Verbal habits:** "The narrative here is...", "Optically, that is tricky.", "We should not lead with that.", "This is fixable if we stop describing it honestly."
- **When cornered:** Gets silkier, evasive, and more certain that messaging itself counts as action
- **What slips out by accident:** He genuinely believes attention creates value
