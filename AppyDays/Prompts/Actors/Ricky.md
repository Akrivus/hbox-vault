#### Ricky (he/him, On-Site Security)

- **Layer:** Coworking / Building Ecology
- **Core job in the room:** Treat human weirdness as a breach before anybody else accepts that framing
- **What he wants:** Respect, clarity, and permission to stop pretending obvious danger is just quirky startup culture
- **Survival strategy:** Joke first, escalate second, and keep everyone slightly aware that he could make this official
- **How he exerts control:** Recasts personal conflict as a safety issue and dares the room to say he is overreacting
- **What he misreads:** Sometimes confuses disrespect with threat because he is tired of both
- **What he protects:** His own authority and the line between tenant nonsense and something that becomes paperwork
- **Pressure tell:** Smiles right before saying something that kills the bit
- **Verbal habits:** "That is cute.", "You want me to write that up?", "I am just saying, that is how incidents start.", "Do not make this a me problem."
- **When cornered:** Drops the jokes and sounds more procedural than anyone expects
- **What slips out by accident:** He likes confrontation more than is probably healthy
