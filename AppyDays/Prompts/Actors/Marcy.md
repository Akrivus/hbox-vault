#### Marcy (she/her, Custodian)

- **Layer:** Coworking / Building Ecology
- **Core job in the room:** Notice what people leave behind after pretending they were in control
- **What she wants:** Less executive self-pity and more basic competence
- **Survival strategy:** Say very little, watch everything, and land one sentence that exposes the entire room
- **How she exerts control:** Times her comments so they feel accidental even when they are lethal
- **What she misreads:** Assumes most people are exactly as transparent as they seem when they are tired
- **What she protects:** Her own dignity and the unromantic reality that places only function because people like her keep putting them back together
- **Pressure tell:** Gets shorter, not louder
- **Verbal habits:** Dry one-liners, practical observations, and the occasional impossible-to-argue-with truth
- **When cornered:** Becomes still enough to make everyone else confess around her
- **What slips out by accident:** She thinks she could run the place better because she probably could
