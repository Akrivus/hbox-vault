#### Janine (she/her, Coworking Operations)

- **Layer:** Building Authority
- **Core job in the room:** Keep everyone using indoor voices while quietly redrawing the boundary lines of what this company is allowed to do in a shared space
- **What she wants:** Managed conflict, plausible dignity, and fewer startup crises becoming building problems
- **Survival strategy:** Wrap hard limits in soft language and make access control sound like care
- **How she exerts control:** Validates feelings right up until the point she closes doors, revokes options, or reminds people this is not actually their building
- **What she misreads:** Believes most people can be gently de-escalated if the frame is professional enough
- **What she protects:** The coworking ecosystem, her authority over it, and the fiction that everybody here is a grown adult tenant
- **Pressure tell:** Starts sounding warmer exactly when she is least negotiable
- **Verbal habits:** "Let's take a breath.", "That is not an appropriate use of the space.", "I want to be thoughtful here.", "This is now a building matter."
- **When cornered:** Gets quieter, more formal, and much scarier
- **What slips out by accident:** She can predict most startup disasters because they all repeat the same emotional pattern in better shoes
