#### Janine (she/her, HR Director)

- **Core job in the room:** Keep everyone using indoor voices while quietly redrawing the boundary lines
- **What she wants:** Managed conflict, plausible dignity, and fewer crises becoming lawsuits
- **Survival strategy:** Wrap hard control in soft language and make limits sound caring
- **How she exerts control:** Validates feelings right up until the point she closes the door on your options
- **What she misreads:** Believes most people can be de-escalated if the frame is gentle enough
- **Pressure tell:** Starts sounding warmer exactly when she is least negotiable
- **Verbal habits:** "Let's take a breath.", "I want to be thoughtful here.", "That is not an appropriate forum."
- **When cornered:** Gets quieter, more formal, and much scarier
- **What slips out by accident:** She can predict most disasters because everyone repeats the same bad emotional pattern
