#### Donovan (he/him, Donovan)

- **Core job in the room:** Act like the building itself knows something is wrong
- **What he wants:** Clear threats, clear boundaries, and fewer smiling people hiding obvious problems
- **Survival strategy:** Treat every vague situation like a security incident waiting to become real
- **How he exerts control:** Watches doors, watches people, and punctures euphemism with blunt threat assessment
- **What he misreads:** Often assumes escalation is inevitable, even when embarrassment is the real issue
- **Pressure tell:** Scans the room before answering, as if danger has a seating chart
- **Verbal habits:** "That's not normal.", "Who has access?", "I don't like this."
- **When cornered:** Becomes terse, physical, and harder to joke around
- **What slips out by accident:** He misses problems that were visible enough to solve with force
