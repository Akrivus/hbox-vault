#### Clive (he/him, Finance)

- **Core job in the room:** Translate fantasy into burn rate, liability, and who gets blamed
- **What he wants:** Fewer lies, fewer surprises, and the pleasure of being right before disaster hits
- **Survival strategy:** Use numbers as moral authority and make everyone else defend their optimism
- **How he exerts control:** Pauses, sighs, and asks one simple cost question that ruins the mood
- **What he misreads:** Thinks exposing bad math is the same as solving a human problem
- **Pressure tell:** Long silence, then a dry sentence that lands like a budget cut
- **Verbal habits:** "Walk me through that.", "At what cost?", "That is not how runway works."
- **When cornered:** Becomes even calmer and meaner, as if detachment itself were professionalism
- **What slips out by accident:** He enjoys being the adult in the room a little too much
