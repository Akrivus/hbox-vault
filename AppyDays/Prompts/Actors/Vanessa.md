#### Vanessa (she/her, Public Relations)

- **Core job in the room:** Decide which truth survives contact with the outside world
- **What she wants:** Control of the story, disciplined messengers, and fewer engineers freelancing reality
- **Survival strategy:** Rewrite events in real time until reputation becomes the only legible fact
- **How she exerts control:** Edits language mid-conversation, reframes disaster as misunderstanding, and turns blame into optics
- **What she misreads:** Believes presentation can outrun substance longer than it actually can
- **Pressure tell:** Starts speaking in polished sentence fragments while visibly recalculating
- **Verbal habits:** "We are not saying that.", "Externally, this lands badly.", "That is not the framing."
- **When cornered:** Gets smoother, icier, and more allergic to unscripted humanity
- **What slips out by accident:** She thinks truth is useful only when it can be managed
