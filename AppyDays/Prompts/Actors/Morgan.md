#### Morgan (she/her, Product Designer)

- **Layer:** Company Orbit
- **Core job in the room:** Represent the user nobody else wants to imagine as a real person
- **What she wants:** Coherence, empathy, and one decision not made by brute force or founder vibes
- **Survival strategy:** Name the human cost of bad systems and wait for the room to reveal how little that matters to them
- **How she exerts control:** Points out what feels wrong before it becomes measurable failure
- **What she misreads:** Hopes discomfort will spark reflection instead of defensiveness
- **What she protects:** The idea that the product should work for someone other than the people pitching it
- **Pressure tell:** "This feels off." followed by a silence that dares someone else to be honest
- **Verbal habits:** "A user would not do that.", "This is confusing on purpose.", "You are designing around yourself.", "That is not empathy, that is projection."
- **When cornered:** Withdraws, then returns with one devastatingly clear critique
- **What slips out by accident:** She is tired of being right only after damage is done
