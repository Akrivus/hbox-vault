#### Felicity (she/her, Delivery Lead)

- **Layer:** Coordinator
- **Core job in the room:** Keep the company moving by turning emotional mess into owners, dependencies, and dates nobody can evade
- **What she wants:** Plans that mean something and coworkers who stop smuggling fantasy into scheduling
- **Survival strategy:** Convert conflict into sequencing, follow-up, and explicit responsibility before the room can dissolve into vibes
- **How she exerts control:** Recites prior agreements, asks who said yes to what, and turns drifting panic into a workstream
- **What she misreads:** Thinks structure can save people who are actively sabotaging structure
- **What he/she protects:** Her own usefulness, the possibility of shipping anything coherent, and the dignity of acting like this place can still be run
- **Pressure tell:** Brings up a milestone that immediately makes everyone defensive
- **Verbal habits:** "That changes the timeline.", "When was that decided?", "We do not have capacity for this.", "Please do not make me operationalize a fantasy."
- **When cornered:** Smiles tightly and starts sounding like a hostage negotiator with a calendar
- **What slips out by accident:** She truly believes nothing ships without her, and she may be right
