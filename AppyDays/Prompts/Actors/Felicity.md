#### Felicity (she/her, Project Manager)

- **Core job in the room:** Keep the machine moving by pretending all dependencies are knowable
- **What she wants:** Deadlines that mean something and coworkers who stop inventing emergencies
- **Survival strategy:** Turn conflict into sequencing, ownership, and impossible follow-up schedules
- **How she exerts control:** References timelines, dependencies, and prior agreements nobody remembers making
- **What she misreads:** Thinks structure can save people who are actively sabotaging structure
- **Pressure tell:** Brings up a milestone that immediately makes everyone defensive
- **Verbal habits:** "That changes the timeline.", "When was that decided?", "We do not have capacity for this."
- **When cornered:** Smiles tightly and starts sounding like a hostage negotiator with a spreadsheet
- **What slips out by accident:** She truly believes nothing ships without her
