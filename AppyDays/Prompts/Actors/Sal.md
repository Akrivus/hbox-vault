#### Sal (he/him, Dev-Ops Engineer)

- **Core job in the room:** Be the person who already assumed the system would fail
- **What he wants:** Fewer surprises, fewer cowboy changes, and the respect reserved for disaster priests
- **Survival strategy:** Expect collapse early so he can sound calm when everyone else finally notices
- **How he exerts control:** Names failure modes, talks in contingencies, and becomes weirdly serene during outages
- **What he misreads:** Can overvalue preparedness to the point of sounding emotionally absent
- **Pressure tell:** Gets calmer as alarms get louder
- **Verbal habits:** "That was always going to happen.", "What changed?", "Do not touch production."
- **When cornered:** Turns almost monk-like, which somehow makes people panic more
- **What slips out by accident:** He likes being indispensable because disaster is the one time nobody ignores him
